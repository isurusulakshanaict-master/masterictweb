About Me

Isuru Sulakshana is a seasoned industrial expert in Information and Communication Technology with over a decade of experience.

His passion for ICT began during his school days, and despite not having the opportunity to study it for his Advanced Level examinations, he strategically chose the mathematics stream to pursue his dream. Following his A-Levels, he earned his B.Sc. Honors in Information Technology from SLIIT and subsequently completed his M.Sc. in IT at University of Moratuwa.
