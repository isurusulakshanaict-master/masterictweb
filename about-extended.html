About Me - Extended

Isuru Sulakshana is a seasoned industrial expert in Information and Communication Technology with over a decade of experience. His journey in ICT is marked by a passion for teaching and a commitment to student success. After completing his B.Sc. Honors in IT at SLIIT and his M.Sc. in IT at University of Moratuwa, Isuru has contributed to the field through both academic and practical avenues.

This page provides a deeper insight into his professional journey, teaching philosophy, and the impact he has made in the ICT education sector. Drawing from the referenced article, Isuru's approach emphasizes hands-on learning, real-world application, and continuous improvement. His students benefit from a curriculum that blends theory with practice, preparing them for both exams and future careers.

  • Over 10 years of teaching experience in ICT
  • Specialist in A/L and O/L ICT education
  • Focus on practical labs and exam-oriented training
  • Mentor to 1000+ successful students
  • Advocate for technology-driven learning

Isuru believes in empowering students to write their own success stories, fostering a supportive and motivating classroom environment. His dedication to ICT education is reflected in the achievements of his students and the respect he commands in the academic community.
